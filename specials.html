MAKE-A-WISH

First Row has aligned with Make-A-Wish® to raise money to help grant the wishes of children with life-threatening medical conditions. From November 24, 2014 - January 4, 2015 guests will be able to buy a star for $1 and select the coupon of their choice, with all proceeds benefiting Make-A-Wish. The coupon choices are $5 of FREE Game Play (with purchase of $10 game play) or a Free Appetizer (with the purchase of an entrée), to be used on a return visit to Dave & Buster's between January 5 - February 1, 2015. Join us to help grant wishes for these courageous kids.
