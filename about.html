About Us

Experience the most thrilling match-ups played out in supersize at First Row Sports Bar & Grill, where bigger the better is our mantra. Why? Because we're derived from the dreams of sports fans like you who dared to want a more brilliant place to cheer for the good guys.

Watch the most exciting moments of your game pulse through our 39-foot HD Big Screen and 199 HD TVs. Sip over 40 beer selections from over 126 draught taps. Indulge in 14 award-winning wing flavours and 11 mouth-watering burgers. Soak it all up in our 25,000 sqft of incredible atmosphere.

Our Story +

Walk through the doors from the sprawling parking lot on any given day or evening and you’ll be pleasantly surprised at the unique atmosphere in First Row Sports Bar Grill.

Since it opened just over 5 years ago, First Row stands strong – and alone. They’re not just a bar. First Row is a restaurant and entertainment experience that thrives on creating great food at terrific values for all their guests.
